BAKER'S CHRISTMAS MENU

We bake handmade bread, pastries, and cakes throughout the day in all our bakeries served with our hand roastedbr
speciality grade house blend coffee.

Our festive menu is packed with comfort and joy. From our Christmas Buns made with buttery brioche dough, filled
with frangipane and topped with zesty vanilla icing, to our Mince Pies packed with brandy-soaked fruit and
encased in soft, crumbly pastry, we've got Christmas covered.

This year, we've given our Turkey & Cheese Sandwich a twist. Made with our beloved Waste-less Sourdough, delicately
smoked turkey from Walter Rose & Son and creamy Mayfield Swiss cheese, we've topped off the crown jewels
with sweet bacon jam and smoky chipotle aioli for good measure.

Discover the joys of our Christmas menu in bakeries now.
